Potato Bacon Soup

Return to main page.

Ingredients

Pre-cook:
1 lb Potatoes, cooked and chopped to your preferred size
1 lb Bacon, cooked and chopped
¼ cup Butter
1 Onion, medium-sized and diced
2 Carrots, medium-sized and sliced
½ tsp. Salt
½ tsp. Pepper
2 Celery stalks, sliced
2 Garlic cloves, minced
8 oz. Cream cheese, room temperature (one package)
1 can Cream of mushroom soup
1½ cups Milk
2 cups Chicken broth/potato water
½ tsp. Cayenne pepper (or to taste)
1 tsp. Pepper (or to taste)
1 tsp. Parsley flakes
1 (15 oz.) Whole kernel corn (with water)

Directions

Boil potatoes in well-salted water until soft; reserve water. Pre-cook bacon, chop and set aside.

Melt the butter in a large soup-pot. Add carrots, onion, celery, and season with salt and pepper. Cook until softened. Add minced garlic and saute until fragrant. Set aside in bowl with the bacon.

Whisk creamcheese in pot on medium/medium-low heat until smooth. Add mushroom soup, whisking until combined. Increase heat to medium/medium-high. Add milk in batches while whisking mixture until combined. Add broth/potato-water while stirring.

Add remaining seasoning as well as the reserved bacon & veggies. Pour in corn along with its water. Bring to a low boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes. Serve.

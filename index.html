Potato Soup

Dave has a love for hamburgers and Asian Cuisines. His sweet-tooth is renowned in our house, and everyone knows to get their sweets when they can because it may not still be there when you get back! (He'll kill me for saying that! 😂)

While Dave loves to make the chocolate chip cookies & cinnamon buns dissapear, he commonly requests a big pot of potato soup paired with French Bread. Those suppers are no-dessert nights, there is no room for sweets after this hearty meal!
